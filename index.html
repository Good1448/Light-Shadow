About the Project

The Bonk Light & Shadow Art project explores the captivating interplay between traditional paper-cut techniques and modern lighting technology. Our artists combine precision-cut paper designs with strategic lighting to create stunning visual contrasts that transform static art into dynamic experiences.

Each artwork in our collection tells a unique story through the manipulation of light and shadow. By layering different densities of paper cuts and experimenting with various light angles, we create depth, movement, and narrative in our installations.

Concept

Exploring the relationship between absence and presence through negative space

Technique

Traditional paper-cutting combined with modern digital design tools

Layered Contrast

Static Contrast Series

About This Work

This piece demonstrates the interplay between two layers of paper-cut art with varying densities. The top layer features bold, structural outlines, while the bottom layer adds intricate details that emerge through strategic lighting.

By using dual light sources from above and to the side, we create a dynamic contrast between sharp, defined shadows and soft, diffused light patterns.

Materials

Hand-cut archival paper, LED lighting

Dimensions

120cm × 80cm × 30cm

Year

2025
